The Game

The only dice game you must have!

This is a nice little app that lets you roll dice.

Are you playing a game, but can't find the dice?

This app is your friend.

If you want to play the game of dices, first you have to Sign Up an account.

If you already have an account you have to log in to play.

Object of the game

Beat your own high score.

Skills learned

Playing patiently and with rhythm.

How to play

You have 6 different dices with numbers 1 2 3 4 5 6.

You have to roll the dice 10 times.

After you roll the dice you can start a new game.

ATTENTION!

This game is based on the original rules of the classic dice game.

No additional cups or dice rolling - the only thing that counts is your skill (...and a little bit of luck ;))!

A quick view

Click the button to start playing.

On your right corner are your points for each roll of the dice.

At the bottom of the page is the player's name.

If you don't want to play you can simply sign out.

Your scores are saved in our database system.
